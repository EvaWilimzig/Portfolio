Unhyper, students!

You've been so busy, hyper student...

For the student body of my school and for a class called "The Hyper Student" by Susanne Stahl, I designed a series of screen printed accordion-fold brochures intending to help Hyper Students / workaholics take a moment to appreciate their work and relax. It is covered in illustrations of our campus, texts I wrote myself and quotes that simply fit. The illustrations are also designed to be postcards, that can be torn out.
