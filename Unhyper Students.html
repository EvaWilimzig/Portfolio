Unhyper, students!

You've been so busy, hyper student...

For the student body of my school I designed a series of Leporellos intending to help Hyper Students / workaholics take a moment to apprechiate their work and relax. It is covered in illustrations of our campus, text I wrote myself and quotes that fit. They are also designed to be postcards, that can be torn out.
