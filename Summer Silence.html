Summer Silence

Summer Silence is a silent picture book inspired by the chapter Dead Calm in Tove Jansson's “Summerbook”.

This picture book was created in the class “Island In The Sun” by Lisa Bucher at the FHP and describes the happenings of a chapter in Tove Jansson's “Summerbook”. Tove Jansson is an author and artist best known for The Moomins.

A boat floats on a calm sea bearing family of three beneath a violet parasol. The complex relationship of a grandmother to her granddaughter. The boredom and excitement of small moments spend on an island in the sun.
