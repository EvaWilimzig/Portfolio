Art World Conflict

Art World Conflict is a excerpt from "Art in a conflicted world" by DCV

"Art World Conflict" is a exerpt from the catalogue "Art in a conflicted world" which was published 2021 by DCV. It features works by the Floyd Corporation, Sarah Dobai and Tanya B Zaharchenko. This project was used as practice in editorial design and shows my own interpretation of the topic.
