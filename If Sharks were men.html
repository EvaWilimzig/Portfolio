If Sharks were Men

“If Sharks were Men” is a picture book based on a text by Bertolt Brecht.

This picture book was created in the class “Experimentelle Strategien im Grafikdesign” by Sven Völker at the FHP and works with a text by Bertolt Brecht criticising society.
